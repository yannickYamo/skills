## Roadmap Health Check

Before sharing:

- [ ] Ties to strategy canvas (not random features)
- [ ] Outcomes > outputs (metrics, not just deliverables)
- [ ] Trade-offs visible (what we're NOT doing)
- [ ] Confidence levels honest (not everything is "high")
- [ ] No false date precision (quarters, not weeks)
- [ ] Updated within last 30 days
- [ ] Team has seen and agrees
