## Launch Tier Definitions

| Tier | Definition | GTM Effort |
|------|------------|------------|
| **Tier 1** | Major new capability, strategic priority | Full GTM: press, event, campaign, all enablement |
| **Tier 2** | Significant improvement, notable customer value | Moderate GTM: blog, email, sales brief, docs |
| **Tier 3** | Incremental improvement, quality of life | Light GTM: changelog, in-app, support brief |
| **Tier 4** | Bug fix, minor enhancement | Minimal: release notes only |
