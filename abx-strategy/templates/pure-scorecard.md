### Scoring Guide

**Painful (0-3):** How acute is the problem?
- 0 = Mild inconvenience, workaround exists
- 1 = Notable pain, but manageable
- 2 = Significant pain, actively looking for solutions
- 3 = Severe pain, causing real damage today

**Urgent (0-3):** Is there a forcing function?
- 0 = No timeline, "someday" project
- 1 = General priority, within next year
- 2 = Active project with defined timeline
- 3 = Imminent deadline, mandate, or event

**Recognized (0-3):** Does the buyer know they have this problem?
- 0 = Unaware of the problem
- 1 = Vaguely aware but not prioritized
- 2 = Recognized and exploring options
- 3 = Actively seeking solution, budget allocated

**Expensive (0-3):** What's the cost of inaction?
- 0 = Low/negligible cost to delay
- 1 = Some cost, but absorbable
- 2 = Material cost (financial, competitive, strategic)
- 3 = Existential risk, major strategic impact
