## Strategy Quality Checklist

Before sharing, verify:

- [ ] Non-goals are explicit and meaningful

- [ ] Anti-ICP exists and is enforced
- [ ] Moat thesis is articulated
- [ ] Pricing is intentional, not defaulted
- [ ] GTM motion is chosen (not "all of them")
- [ ] Bets have kill criteria
- [ ] Everything fits on this page (when formatted)
